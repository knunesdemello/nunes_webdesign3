Our Philosophy

As an eco-friendly online marketplace, Candid has done the work so you don’t have to. Here, you can pick from products that are sourced, manufactured, and shipped in a way that protects our planet’s future. Each brand we’ve partnered with has been chosen because they’re taking the right steps and helping to create a more sustainable future. Our goal is to make buying responsibly second-nature. We’re on a mission to make it normal to consume in a way that’s easier on the environment, while meeting the convenience we’ve come to expect. But this is not a story about Candid.This is a story about all of us.
